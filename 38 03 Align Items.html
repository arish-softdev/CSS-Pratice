Align Items


Understanding "align-items" in CSS Flexbox

  • The "align-items" property is used to align flex items along the cross-axis of the flex container.
  • It determines how flex items are positioned vertically within the container.

The "align-items" Property

  1. flex-start:Items are aligned at the start of the cross axis.
  2. flex-end:Items are aligned at the end of the cross axis.
  3. center:Items are centered along the cross axis.

1. Flex Start Alignment

  • Items are aligned at the top of the container with `align-items: flex-start`.
