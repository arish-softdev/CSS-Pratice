Flex Wrap


Understanding "flex-wrap" in CSS Flexbox

  • The "flex-wrap" property controls whether flex items are forced onto one line or can wrap onto multiple lines.
  • It determines how flex items should behave when they overflow the flex container's size.

The "flex-wrap" Property

  1. nowrap: Items are fit to a single line (no wrapping).
  2. wrap: Items wrap around to additional lines as needed.
  3. wrap-reverse: Items wrap around to additional lines in reverse order.

1. No Wrap Flexbox Layout

  • The `flex-wrap: nowrap` property ensures that flex items stay on a single line without wrapping to the next line.
