Align Self


Understanding "align-self" in CSS Flexbox

  • The "align-self" property is used to control the alignment of individual flex items within a flex container.
  • It allows each item to be aligned differently along the cross-axis, overriding the container's "align-items" property.

The "align-self" Property

  • flex-start: Item is aligned at the start of the cross axis.
  • flex-end: Item is aligned at the end of the cross axis.
  • center: Item is centered along the cross axis.
  • stretch: Item is stretched to fill the cross-axis.
