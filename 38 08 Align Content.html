Understanding "align-content" in CSS Flexbox

  • The "align-content" property is used to align multiple lines of flex items along the cross-axis of the flex container.
  • It determines how the space between and around flex lines is distributed in the container.

The "align-content" Property

  1. flex-start: Lines are packed at the start of the container.
  2. flex-end: Lines are packed at the end of the container.
  3. center: Lines are centered in the container.
  4. space-between: Lines are evenly distributed with the first line at the start and the last line at the end.
  5. space-around: Lines are evenly distributed with equal space around them.
  6. stretch: Lines are stretched to fit the container.
